Familia Gastro S.L.

Familia Gastro S.L. is a Galician company specialized in fresh ready-made meals with added value. It combines technology, tradition, and efficiency to serve both business and retail markets with a high-quality gastronomic offering.

Corporate Foodservice

Delicious meals ready for offices and companies, combining taste, quality, and convenience.

Supermarket Production

High-End Gastronomy for Hospitality

Gourmet line designed to elevate the experience in restaurants and hotels with top-level culinary proposals.
