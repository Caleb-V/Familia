Familia Gastro S.L.

Familia Gastro S.L. is a Galician company specialized in fresh ready-made meals with added value. It combines technology, tradition, and efficiency to serve both the corporate and retail markets with a quality gastronomic proposal.

We Are Specialists

Corporate Foodservice

Delicious ready-made dishes for offices and companies, combining taste, quality, and practicality.

Supermarket Production

We offer ready-to-sell products, with private label and white label options, ensuring freshness and excellence.

Menus for Seniors

Tailored nutrition for nursing homes and public services, ensuring well-being and balanced diets.

High-End Gastronomy for Hospitality

Gourmet line designed to elevate the experience in restaurants and hotels with top-tier culinary offerings.
